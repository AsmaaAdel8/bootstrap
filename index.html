rose types

In general, roses are classified by 3 main categories – modern garden roses, wild roses, and old garden roses. There are over 300 species of roses in total, with lower classifications of roses totaling thousands of cultivars and hybrids.

types of roses in the real-world

The classification of modern roses includes all roses that have been cultivated since 1867. The first of the modern roses was a hybrid developed by Jean-Baptiste Guillot

Old garden roses are classified as any type of rose that was cultivated or existed before 1867 when the first modern rose was created.
“Species roses” is the botanical name for roses that grow naturally in the wild and are not the result of hybridization or cultivation.
